Loaded Nachos with Corned Beef

Published May 10, 2024

Nachos are not like any other nachos you’ve tried before, as they are made with crispy and golden brown potato chips, topped with tender and juicy corned beef, melted cheese, and a variety of other toppings that will leave you craving for more.

These loaded nachos with corned beef are perfect for sharing with friends or enjoying as a tasty snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag (about 200g) corn tortilla chips
  • 1 can (200g) corned beef, shredded
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup sliced jalapenos
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • Lime wedges for serving
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Spread the corn tortilla chips in a single layer on a large baking sheet.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheese evenly over the chips.
  4. Top with shredded corned beef, diced tomatoes, diced red onion, sliced jalapenos, and sliced black olives.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven and sprinkle chopped fresh cilantro over the top.
  7. Drizzle sour cream and salsa over the nachos.
  8. Top with diced avocado and serve immediately with lime wedges on the side.
  9. Enjoy your loaded nachos with corned beef!
