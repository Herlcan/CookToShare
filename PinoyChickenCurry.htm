Pinoy Chicken Curry

Published May 10, 2024

This Pinoy Chicken Curry recipe is the Filipino version of the popular chicken curry dish. It is delicious and easy to cook. The sauce is rich, creamy, and flavorful. It goes well with warm white rice.

This hearty and flavorful Filipino-style chicken curry is a comfort food favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 carrot, peeled and sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook until softened and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the pot and cook until lightly browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Stir in the curry powder and cook for another 2 minutes to toast the spices.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and fish sauce. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the cubed potatoes and sliced carrot to the pot. Cover and simmer for another 10 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Stir in the sliced red and green bell peppers. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Remove from heat and transfer to a serving dish.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired.
  10. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Pinoy Chicken Curry!
