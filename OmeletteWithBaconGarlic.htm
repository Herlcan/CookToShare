Omelette with Bacon and Garlic

Published May 10, 2024

This omelet is big enough to serve four, but it still has the merits of an individual omelet: tender not tough eggs and a hearty filling that doesn't weigh down the eggs.

This delicious omelet is packed with flavor from crispy bacon and aromatic garlic.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or your favorite cheese)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Fresh chives for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy. Remove from the skillet and drain on paper towels. Crumble the bacon into small pieces and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, minced garlic, shredded cheese, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat the butter in the same skillet over medium heat. Pour in the egg mixture and cook until the edges start to set, about 2 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle the crumbled bacon evenly over the omelet. Continue cooking until the eggs are set but still slightly runny in the center, about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Using a spatula, fold the omelet in half. Cook for another minute to melt the cheese and finish cooking the eggs.
  6. Slide the omelet onto a plate and garnish with fresh chives if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!
