Peach Mango Pie (Air Fried)

Published May 10, 2024

Peach Mango Pie with mouth-watering tropical filling and golden, flaky pie crust. Enjoy Jollibee’s popular pocket pie at home with this homemade recipe.

This delicious dessert combines the sweetness of peach and mango in a crispy crust, cooked to perfection in an air fryer.

Ingredients:

  • 2 sheets puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 cup diced peaches
  • 1 cup diced mangoes
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar (for dusting)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine diced peaches, diced mangoes, granulated sugar, cornstarch, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
  3. Roll out the puff pastry sheets on a lightly floured surface. Cut each sheet into squares, about 4x4 inches in size.
  4. Place a spoonful of the peach mango filling in the center of each puff pastry square.
  5. Brush the edges of the puff pastry squares with beaten egg, then fold them over to form triangles. Press the edges firmly to seal.
  6. Brush the tops of the pies with more beaten egg for a golden finish.
  7. Place the pies in the air fryer basket, making sure they are not touching each other. You may need to cook them in batches depending on the size of your air fryer.
  8. Cook the pies in the air fryer for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.
  9. Remove the pies from the air fryer and let them cool slightly.
  10. Dust the tops of the pies with powdered sugar before serving.
  11. Serve warm and enjoy your Peach Mango Pies!
